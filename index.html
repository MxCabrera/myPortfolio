About Me

Hey, thank you for stopping by to read this! I'm Miguel (he/him). I'm a front end developer with major passions for building and creating. I graduated from Juno College immersive Bootcamp for Web Development. From this experience, I've learned and gained a strong understanding in coding languages, which has allowed me to create responsive, user-friendly, clean and accessible web apps with dreams of eventually becoming a Full Stack Developer.

Prior to this bootcamp, I graduated with a background in Architectural Technology & shortly after worked for a tech company that specialized in 3D modeling & Virtual Reality (VR/AR). Working there taught me how to respond efficiently when working in a high pressure environment. I've also learned to become an adaptable team member and communicate well in a group or pair.

Outside of code, I enjoy playing basketball, sketching, and hanging out with my Border Collie, Kaia. With these experiences, I have grown dedicated to my craft and aim to create websites that provide clients with intuitive designs, functionality and accessibility. Coding has allowed me to express my creativity and develop a strong foundation to build on. I am excited to begin this new journey with a positive attitude and an open mindset.
